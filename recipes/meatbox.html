Meatbox

Description

This homemade version of rotisserie Doner Kebab Meat can be made with either lamb or beef. While beef is a firm favourite here in Australia, in Turkey (the home of Doner Kebabs) they are made with both lamb and beef. Both are delicious, I couldn’t choose a favourite!

Ingredients

  • Beef or Lamb
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Spices
  • Duck Fat

Steps

  1. Turn meat out onto work surface. Wet hands with water, then shape into an even block 20cm/8" long.
  2. Cook for 1 1/2 hours, turning once after 1 hour, until the log reaches 70°C/160°F
  3. Remove skewers then stand the meat upright.
  4. Smear hummus on warmed flatbread. Top with lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  5. Roll up tightly, wrap in foil if desired (to hold together). Grab and devour!
